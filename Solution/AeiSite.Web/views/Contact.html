Company

Aesthetic Interactive was founded by Zach Kinstner and is located in Grand Rapids, Michigan. With beginnings in 2005, Zach officially started the development and design company in 2007 (upon college graduation).

Since then, Aesthetic Interactive has found continued success, working with teams across the globe on a wide variety of projects. In addition to client work, Aesthetic Interactive undertakes in-house projects and experiments as part of its initiative to push boundaries, sharpen skills, and explore new platforms.

Founder

Zach Kinstner earned a Computer Science degree from Grand Valley State University's Honors College in 2007. Zach is passionate about integrating art and technology, exploring new concepts, building software, and creating new user experiences. His wife and two daughters are pretty awesome, too.
